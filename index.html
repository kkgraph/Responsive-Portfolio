About Me

After calling France, Australia, and Quebec home, she finally settled down in Brooklyn with her much-loved dog, Dr. Boo. She loves horror movies, doodling, devouring books, and believes Montreal bagels are superior to New York bagels (#SorryNotSorry).

Under the pen name, Jamie Mae, she has a mystery chapter book series coming out from little bee publisher in 2019. The first two books came out in March with the next book due out in May and the final one around Christmas.
